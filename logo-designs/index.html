// Market Study — What Great Logos Do

▲ VERCEL

Pure Geometry

A single filled triangle. Zero ornament. Works at 12px favicon and on a billboard. The lesson: the best logos are shapes a child could draw from memory.

◇ LINEAR

Functional Precision

An abstract L in a circle that reads like a precision drafting tool. It communicates the product promise (speed, no friction) through form alone.

⚡ SUPABASE

Kinetic Energy

Intersecting lightning bolts = speed + power. The bright green cuts through corporate blue. High-contrast accent colors demand attention on screen.

⌘ RAYCAST

The Command Interface

An abstract cursor/beam that suggests "launch anything." For a personal OS, the logo should feel like a launcher — a portal to everything.

◎ ARC BROWSER

Crafted Personality

A tilted, colorful arc. It breaks from the sterile circle of every other browser. For AIIMIN: don't default to safe geometry. Show personality.

▣ NOTION

Dimensional Warmth

An isometric 3D "N" with a pencil stroke. It feels human, approachable. AIIMIN needs this balance — technical precision + emotional warmth.

⬡ STRIPE

Diagonal Momentum

Two diagonal slashes suggest forward motion and speed of transactions. Diagonal lines universally read as "movement" — a powerful tool for a growth app.

★ AIIMIN BRIEF

What Our Logo Must Do

Communicate: personal OS, multi-domain life, ambition, system-level control, growth, daily ritual. Work at: 16px favicon, 32px tab, 80px navbar, 200px+ splash.
