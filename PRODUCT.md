# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students and early-career builders under high cognitive load. They open AIIMIN to log life data, stay consistent, and recover from friction — not to perform wellness theater.

## Product Purpose

Personal Life OS: metrics, money, calendar, focus, discipline, journal, notes, sports context. Success = low-friction capture, truthful patterns, coaching language without clinical claims.

## Brand Personality

Calm · precise · honest. Practical coaching over vanity analytics. Orange accent signals action; never shame chrome.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI purple/indigo SaaS landings
- Warm cream + terracotta “editorial AI” look
- Broadsheet dense newspaper dashboards
- Gamified recovery (streak shame, badges on urge data)
- Handwriting-canvas / GoodNotes clones in a PWA
- AI therapist personas

## Design Principles

1. Capture cost stays low — intervene during the urge, not only after.
2. Pattern language over punitive streaks.
3. One linking system (AnchorEdge) — no parallel graphs.
4. Design serves the task; familiarity over decorative novelty.
5. Mobile `/m` = capture only; desktop/tablet carry analytics.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim WCAG AA contrast (body ≥4.5:1). Touch targets ≥44px on tablet/phone tiers. Honor `prefers-reduced-motion`. No clinical diagnostic claims in copy.
