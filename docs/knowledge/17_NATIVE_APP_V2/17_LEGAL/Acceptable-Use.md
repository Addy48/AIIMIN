# Acceptable Use Policy & Community Guidelines — AIIMIN

**`[LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]`**

## Acceptable Use

You may not:

1. Violate law or others’ rights  
2. Upload malware or attempt unauthorized access  
3. Scrape, overload, or reverse engineer except as allowed by law  
4. Bypass waitlist, paywalls, or rate limits  
5. Harass, threaten, or share others’ personal data without rights  
6. Use AI features to generate illegal content or clinical advice presented as professional care  
7. Misrepresent affiliation with AIIMIN  

We may suspend or terminate for violations.

## Community Guidelines (if social/wall features exist)

Be respectful · no hate/illegal content · no spam · no graphic self-harm content without crisis framing · report abuse to `[SUPPORT_EMAIL]`. Discipline community walls are for accountability among peers — not therapy.
