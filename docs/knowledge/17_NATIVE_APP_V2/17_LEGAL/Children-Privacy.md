# Children’s Privacy — AIIMIN

**`[LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]`**

AIIMIN is not directed to children under **13** (or **16** where GDPR requires for consent). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the applicable age.

If you believe a child provided data, contact `[SUPPORT_EMAIL]` — we will delete as required.

Parents/guardians in regions with stricter rules: contact us before allowing use. Schools/enterprise youth programs require separate agreements (not offered by default).
