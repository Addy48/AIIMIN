# Data Retention Policy — AIIMIN

**`[LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]`**

| Data category | Retention |
|---------------|-----------|
| Account profile | Life of account + up to 30 days after deletion request (legal holds excepted) |
| User content (logs, habits, journal, notes, etc.) | Life of account; deleted from primary DB within 30 days of account deletion; backups rolling ≤90 days |
| Auth sessions | Until expiry/revoke |
| Security logs | 12–24 months |
| Billing entitlements / invoices | As required by tax law (often 7 years) |
| Analytics events | 14–26 months aggregated/pseudonymous |
| Support tickets | Up to 24 months |
| Waitlist | Until launch conversion or deletion request |
| Soft-deleted sync tombstones | ≤30 days then hard purge |

Deletion requests: You → Data or `[SUPPORT_EMAIL]`. We verify identity. Backups age out on schedule.
