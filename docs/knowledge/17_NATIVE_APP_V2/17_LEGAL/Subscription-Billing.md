**`[LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED]`** · Effective 2026-07-19

## 1. Plans

AIIMIN may offer Free/Explore, Core, Pro, Elite (names may change). Features per plan are described in-product and on the website. Founding pricing may differ from standard.

## 2. Billing

- **Web:** Stripe (or successor). Taxes may apply.  
- **iOS:** Apple In-App Purchase — Apple’s billing terms apply.  
- **Android:** Google Play Billing — Google’s terms apply.  

Entitlements are enforced server-side. Cross-platform access: purchases on one channel generally unlock the account; store rules on refunds still apply to that channel.

## 3. Fulfillment

Digital service access is fulfilled immediately upon successful payment and entitlement activation (usually seconds; up to 24h in rare store delay). No physical shipping.

## 4. Renewal & cancellation

Subscriptions renew until cancelled. Cancel via:

- Web customer portal / account settings  
- Apple Subscriptions settings  
- Google Play Subscriptions  

Cancellation stops future renewal; access continues until end of paid period unless otherwise stated.

## 5. Refunds

- **Store purchases:** Refunds are handled by Apple/Google under their policies; we can assist with entitlement state after store refund.  
- **Web/Stripe:** Request within `[REFUND_WINDOW_DAYS]` days of charge if unused or technical failure prevents access; approved refunds return to original method. Abuse may be denied.  
- Statutory consumer rights (EU/UK/India as applicable) prevail where mandatory.

## 6. Price changes

We may change prices with notice as required by law and store rules. Continued renewal after notice may constitute acceptance where allowed.

## 7. Trials & founding offers

Trials convert to paid unless cancelled. Founding rates may be limited and non-transferable.

Unjustified chargebacks may lead to suspension pending review.
