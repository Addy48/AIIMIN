Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how AIIMIN ("we," "our," or "the Application") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our personal productivity dashboard at www.aiimin.in.

AIIMIN is a private, personal productivity dashboard. By using AIIMIN, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data: When you sign in using Google OAuth, we receive your basic Google profile information — your name, email address, and profile picture. This is used solely to create and maintain your account.

Dashboard Data: Daily log entries, goals, focus sessions, journal notes, habit records, finance data, and other productivity information you manually enter are stored securely and used exclusively to power your personal dashboard experience.

Google Calendar Data: If you connect Google Calendar, AIIMIN reads your calendar events using the Google Calendar API (read-only). Calendar data is fetched in real time to display your schedule. It is NOT permanently stored on our servers.

YouTube Data: If you connect YouTube, AIIMIN reads your YouTube activity using the YouTube Data API (read-only). This data is displayed inside your dashboard and is NOT permanently stored on our servers.

OAuth Tokens: To maintain Google API connectivity, AIIMIN stores encrypted OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens. These tokens are used only to fulfill API requests you initiate within your dashboard session.

We do not collect advertising data, behavioral profiles, or any third-party analytics. There are no tracking pixels or marketing scripts.

2. Google API Services — Limited Use Policy

AIIMIN's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

(a) Use is limited to providing the service: Google user data is used only to display your own data within your personal AIIMIN dashboard. It is not used for any other purpose.

(b) No transfer to third parties: We do not transfer, sell, share, or disclose Google user data to any third parties except as required to operate the service.

(c) No use for advertising: Google user data is never used to serve advertisements or for any advertising purpose whatsoever.

(d) No use for unrelated purposes: Google user data is not used to train AI or ML models, build behavioral profiles, conduct research, or for any purpose other than displaying your own data inside your own dashboard.

(e) Humans do not read your data: No human — including the developer — accesses your Google account data, except at your explicit request for support or as required by law.

3. Google OAuth Scopes We Request

openid, email, profile — Required to authenticate your identity and create your account. We receive your name, email address, and profile picture.

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.readonly — Read-only access to display your Google Calendar events inside the AIIMIN dashboard. AIIMIN does NOT create, edit, or delete calendar events.

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events — Used for displaying detailed event information within your calendar view. AIIMIN does not create, modify, or delete any calendar events.

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.readonly — Read-only access to display your YouTube activity in your personal dashboard. AIIMIN does NOT post, modify, or delete any YouTube content.

You can revoke any or all permissions at any time via your Google Account Permissions page. Revocation immediately disables the corresponding features.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

• Authenticate your identity and maintain your session securely.

• Display your personal productivity data, calendar events, and YouTube activity inside your dashboard.

• Save and retrieve your manually entered dashboard data (goals, habits, journal entries, etc.).

• Generate personalized AI-powered insights and summaries from your own data.

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal data to any third party. We do not use your data for advertising, behavioral targeting, or any commercial purpose.

5. OAuth Token Storage & Security

OAuth access and refresh tokens obtained through Google authorization are stored in our database with AES encryption at rest. Tokens are:

• Used only to make API calls on your behalf when you are actively using the dashboard.

• Never shared with, sold to, or transferred to any third party.

• Automatically invalidated if you revoke AIIMIN's access via your Google Account.

• Permanently deleted when you delete your account or request data deletion.

6. Data Retention

• Account data (name, email): Retained while your account is active.

• Dashboard data (goals, logs, habits): Retained while your account is active and deleted upon account deletion.

• Google API data (Calendar events, YouTube data): Fetched in real time; not permanently stored.

If you delete your account or request data deletion, all your personal data is permanently purged from our systems within 30 days.

7. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time. To request deletion:

1. Email us at aadityaupadhyay10@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".

2. Or visit our Data Deletion page for self-service options.

All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of a verified deletion request. You can also revoke AIIMIN's Google OAuth access at any time from your Google Account Permissions page.

8. Security Practices

• HTTPS / TLS: All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS.

• Encrypted tokens: OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES encryption.

• Row-Level Security: Database access uses row-level security policies so users can only access their own data.

• Minimal permissions: We request only the minimum OAuth scopes necessary for each feature.

• No password storage: All authentication is handled through Supabase Auth and Google OAuth. We do not store passwords in plaintext.

9. Third-Party Service Providers

• Supabase — Database and authentication infrastructure. Supabase Privacy Policy.

• Vercel — Application hosting and deployment. Vercel Privacy Policy.

• Google APIs — Calendar and YouTube data access for features you explicitly connect. Google Privacy Policy.

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties beyond what is described above.

10. Cookies

AIIMIN uses only essential, functional cookies strictly necessary to operate the service:

• Session cookie (aiimin_session): Maintains your authenticated session. This cookie is essential for you to remain logged in.

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or any non-essential cookies.

11. Your Rights

• Right to access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

• Right to correction: You can update your profile information directly in your dashboard settings.

• Right to deletion: You can request permanent deletion of all your data at any time.

• Right to revoke Google access: You can disconnect AIIMIN from your Google account at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at aadityaupadhyay10@gmail.com.
